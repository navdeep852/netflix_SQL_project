# 📺 Netflix Data Analysis Project

This project explores and analyzes Netflix’s content catalog using SQL and data visualization tools. The goal is to uncover insights about content trends, ratings, genres, and more through structured business questions.

## 🔍 Business Questions Solved

1. Count the number of Movies vs TV Shows  
2. Find the most common rating for Movies and TV Shows  
3. List all movies released in a specific year (e.g., 2020)  
4. Top 5 countries with the most content on Netflix  
5. Identify the longest movie  
6. Find content added in the last 5 years  
7. Find all content by director 'Rajiv Chilaka'  
8. List all TV Shows with more than 5 seasons  
9. Count content in each genre  
10. Top 5 years with the highest average content released in India  
11. List all documentary movies  
12. Find all content without a listed director  
13. Count how many movies Salman Khan appeared in over the past 10 years  
14. Top 10 directors by content count  
15. Monthly content addition trend  
16. Most frequent actors on Netflix  
17. Compare average duration of Movies vs TV Shows  
18. Language-wise content distribution  
19. Peak content addition month  
20. Content type ratio by top countries
